Our Story

Gareth and Kristine met on Kristine's second day of work at Twitter. Since meeting they both became close, spending every hour together. Shortly after they both moved into a studio apartment in San Francisco, sharing arguably the smallest liveable space. The two then moved out of the city to Larkspur, Marin - where they share their love for the outdoors.

On December 2nd, 2016 - Gareth proposed via a scavenger hunt app he built leading to a proposal on Hippy Hill in Belvedue, Marin. Their first date was also a scavenger hunt with capsules buried and hidden around San Francisco.
